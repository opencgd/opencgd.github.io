FAQ

RuneScape is owned and operated by Andrew Gower. I am currently running this game as a hobby in my spare time. Since this is a completely free service I don't have the resources to provide huge amounts of customer support. Instead I have programmed a number of automated systems you can use to help yourself. These enable lost passwords to be recovered, and let you block abusive messages.

PLEASE try to use the built in systems before contacting me. If you still have a problem with an aspect of the game you should stop playing it!

Do NOT email me asking about lost passwords, lost items, or complaining about some aspect of the game you don't like! Email such as these will be ignored! If you absolutely MUST contact me you can do so by emailing me at: runescape@jagex.com
