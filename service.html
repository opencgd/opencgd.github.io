Our technology

Put Java games on your website!

Increase your readership
Online games can vastly increase the average time users spend on your site, and are a great way to ensure users will keep coming back to play their favourite games, improve their scores, and meet the friends they have made on your site. Online games will bring users back again and again. As well as producing a large amount of traffic (and therefore advertising revenue), online games will draw new users to all areas of your site, and are a truly effective way to boost your website's readership.

Make your site interactive
Our multiplayer games allow your visitors to interact with real human opponents, and talk to them with in game chat, and can provide that vital social element which can push a website beyond being just a source of information, into an exciting interactive experience.

Instantly playable
It is essentially to grab a users attention immediately, and make it as easy as possible for them to get involved quickly. All of our games are written in JAVA, which means anyone with a web browser can view and play the games. Just visit the page, and the games will automatically load, and offer users the chance to begin playing immediately without needing to make any downloads or install any plugins!

Customisation available
Our games can be fully branded to your needs. If you require any customisation then let us know what you require and we'll factor it into the cost. If required the text used in the games will be included in a separate modifiable configuration file, so can be altered/translated as you require. If you just want a simple customisation such as the inclusion of your company logo, we can include this at no extra cost.

4 years of experience
We have 4 years of experience developing online games. We've already developed and deployed a large number of games, and so we've already discovered many of the things that can potentially go wrong and made sure they won't! Our software is developed with the benefit of years of experience and hence has the sort of improvements and stability which can only be gained through large scale testing, and user feedback. Something which we believe is hard to obtain from elsewhere.

Pricing
Prices start at a few hundred pounds to license one of our exisiting single-player games, and increase as extra features such as 3D or multiplayer are added. We can also create entirely new games to meet your exact design requirements.
