Clan discussion board
Clans are groups of people who work together and help each other in the RuneScape world. You can use this discussion board to advertise your clan, or to find a clan which you want to join. There is no proper clan support in RuneScape yet, so a lot of (but not all) clans currently require you to create a new character with the clans name at the end. We are however working hard to add proper clan support. Once this this is complete you will be able to join and leave clans more easily.
