Jagex software is recruiting!

Do you want to join our team, to create top of the range online games? We are looking for additional programmers, and artists to work on our huge new multiplayer RPG Runescape, as well as developing a variety of other smaller games like those currently found on our site.

We can provide great recognition for your work. You can expect to see the games you create or work-on, online and being played by thousands of people a day within a matter of months of working for us!

Programmer

As a programmer for Jagex software you will be expected to work independently with lots of creative freedom, drawing on the skills of our graphics artists and other members of the team as required. A love of computer games, and some experience with games programming is required, preferably in either C, C++ or Java.

2d graphics artist

We are looking for skilled artists to create sprites, tiles and textures for our games, using art-software of your choice. You should be experienced at animation, (as sprites in our games typically require 3-24 frames of animation), and capable of the fine detailed work required to create small graphics.

3d graphics artist

We are looking for 3d graphics artists to raytrace sprites and backgrounds using 3d software of your choice. You will also use our custom 3d-modelling software, to create low polygon count models for direct use in our 3d games. You will be expected to be able to create objects using simple primatives, and well chosen textures. Our software also allows models to be created and manipulated on a per-polygon basis, giving a high level of control.

If you are interested, send your CV, and preferably a few samples of your work to:

jobs@jagex.com

All requests are handled confidentially!
