RuneScape members

If you enjoy playing RuneScape then why not support further RuneScape development by becoming a RuneScape member? As a member you will get access to a whole load of additional members-only benefits and features.

Upgrading your chracter to member status costs just $5/month. To sign-up or find out about the different payment options, just Click here!

No-risk! If after trying members RuneScape you decide it's not for you, you can simply switch back to freeplay RuneScape, and no further charges will be made. Of course if you do this any members-only items or skills will be disabled, but they will NOT be removed. This means if you subsequently decide to resume your membership the members items and skills you gained before will start working again.

New features!

Herblaw: The herblaw skill allows you to mix a variety of potions, poisons and antidotes. Everything from super-attack potions to poison to put on the tips of your arrows.

Fletching: The fletching skill allows you to make your own bows and arrows. With 12 different bows to make, and 6 different types of arrows.

Thieving: The thieving skill includes abilities such as pick-pocketing, picking locks, disarming traps, and stealing from market stalls.

Duelling: Duelling allows two players to agree to a fight outside the wilderness. Either for an agreed prize, or just for fun. In addition to agreeing on the stake you can optionally select other options such as: no-retreating, no-prayers, no-magic or even no-weapons!

Sound effects: Optional sound effects are finally available. There are currently 37 different sound effects on actions such as combat, cooking, magic, doors, mining, smithing, etc. Even more sound effects are planned in future updates.

More fishing: Higher level fish to catch. Do you think you've got what it takes to catch a shark? Or perhaps you'll get lucky and net yourself a suprise treasure chest.

More crafting: Try your hand at glass-making, particularly useful if you want to make orbs for your own battlestaff. Or make the powerful dragonstone amulet.

More magic: New teleport spells, New 'wave' projectile spells, Orb enchant spells

Triple size bank: Three times as much bank space to store your items in, and what with all the new members items and features you're going to need it!

New weapons: As well as all the new herbs and potions you can make we've introduce a new range of weapons. Including the dragonstone amulet, battle-staffs, the dragon-sword and the dragon-axe.

 

New quests!

Druidic Ritual: Before you can start using the herblaw skills you must help the druids complete their ritual.

Lost city: Rumour has it that there is a hidden city under the swamp, can you find out how to get in? The fairy market inside apparently has some (literally) out-of-this-world deals!

Witches house: A boy asks you to fetch his ball from behind a small house... This apparently simple task turns out to be much more difficult than expected when you realise the house in question is owned by a particularly crazy witch...

Hero's quest: Prove yourself as a hero by completing this difficult quest! Only once you have completed the multiple challenges given to you, can you enter the hero's guild to buy the new Dragon-axe.

Merlin's crystal: Merlin has been imprisoned inside a giant crystal. Free him and become one of the knight's of the round table.

Scorpion catcher: Thormac the sorcerer will improve your battle staffs if you can find has lost lesser kharid scorpions.

Family crest: The Fitzharmon family crest has gone missing, and the family honour has been lost. Can you find the crest and return it to Dimintheis in Varrock?

Tribal totem: Can you help the rightful owner steal back the lost tribal totem? Requires you to use the new thieving skill.

Monk's friend: Complete several tasks for the monks so they can get a good nights sleep before their annual party.

Fishing contest: Win a fishing contest for the mountain dwarfs to access the underground pass under white-wolf mountain.

Temple of Ikov A mysterious stranger called Lucien asks you to retrieve an artifact known as the Staff of Armadyl.

Clock tower Restore the clock tower south of Ardougne to working order by finding and assembling the missing parts.

 

New Locations!

Wilderness locations: We've filled the wilderness with new exciting places to fight and collect treasure, including the red-dragon lava island, scropion ravine, pirate hall, level-56 wilderness, wilderness dungeons.

Quest locations: We've added a variety of new locations and scenes which as well as providing settings for the new quests are also exciting places to explore and train. These include: Zanaris underground city, Entrana island, Arthur's castle, Fort Grymwold, Ogre dungeon, Barbarian camp, Lord handlemort's mansion.

New towns and villages: Lots of new towns including the pirate town of Brimhaven, Catherby village, the town of Taverley, the huge city of Ardougne, Seers village.

HUGE members dungeon: By far the biggest dungeon in RuneScape, this collosal labyrinth of passageways and caverns becomes more and more dangerous the futher down you go making it the ultimate place to try your combat skills. Includes lots of new powerful monsters and scenary.

 

New Monsters!

Think you can easily beat every monster in the game? Think again!

If the poison scorpions don't get you, then perhaps the level-200 black dragon will! We've added loads of new monsters, and there's something for everyone. As well as new super-strong monsters, there's lots of new opponents for lower level players too.

New monsters include: red-dragon, druid, ice-queen, giant-bat, black-dragon, chaos-druid, white-wolf, baby-dragon, poison-scorpion, ice-spider, black-demon, guard-dog, shape-shifter, blue-dragon, grey-wolf, poison-spider, thug, shadow-spider, ogre, fire-giant, rogue, hero, paladin, other worldy being, black-unicorn, animated axe, hell-hound.

    • Frequently asked questions

    Q: Can I upgrade my existing account? I don't want to have to start a new character!
    A: Yes you can, to become a member you MUST upgrade an exisiting free account. (if you want a new account then just create a new free account and then upgrade it straight away)

    Q: As a member will I be able to play on the free servers still?
    A: Yes you can, although on the free servers none of your members skills or items will work, so we recommend you use the member-only server usually.

    Q: If I stop being a member do I lose all my members items and skills?
    A: No you don't lose them, but they are disabled. If you become a member again they will be re-enabled and you can continue where you left off.

    Q: Do I pay per computer or per account?
    A: You pay per account, you need to pay $5/month for each account you wish to upgrade. You can then use this account from any computer you wish.
